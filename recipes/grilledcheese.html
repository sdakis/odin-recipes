Grilled Cheese Recipe

Grilled Cheese

The grilled cheese is a classic and delicous sandwhich that is super simple to make. However, there are some things to be aware of in order to make a good one.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of white bread
  • two slices of american cheese
  • Butter at room temperature

Steps

  1. Place a pan on low-medium heat. Lower heat ensures the bread toasts more evenly.
  2. Spread the butter on both slices of bread.
  3. Place one slice, butter side down on the pan. Place both slices of cheese on the slice.
  4. Place the other slice of bread, butter side up.
  5. Toast the one side for 5 minutes and flip. Repeat with the other side. For desired color, toast for an additional minute, if necessary.
  6. Remove from pan and cut into triangle.
  7. Serve and enjoy.
