Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs are a simple, nutrious and tasty meal that onyone can make with just a tiny bitof practice.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 3 eggs
  • Salt
  • Green onion

Steps

  1. Place a pan on low-medium heat and let sit.
  2. Chop green onion.
  3. Drop 1 tablespoon of butter onto the pan.
  4. Crack three eggs into a bowl and add salt. Beat the eggs for aproximately 20 seconds.
  5. Pour eggs onto pan and use rubber spatula and stir periodally.
  6. Mix eggs around until cooked.
  7. Place eggs on plate, add salt and chopped green onion.
  8. Serve and enjoy.
